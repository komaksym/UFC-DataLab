See How The Judges Scored Every Round Of UFC 309: Jones vs Miocic, Live From Madison Square Garden In New York City
Nov. 17, 2024

UFC returned to Madison Square Garden with a collision of UFC legends as heavyweight champion Jon Jones defended his crown against former two-time title-holder and current No. 8 ranked Stipe Miocic. In the five-round co-main event, former lightweight champion and No. 2 ranked contender Charles Oliveira battled No. 6 ranked Michael Chandler in a highly anticipated rematch. 

Here's how the judges scored each and every round.
